[head=3]Execution Protocol: Firing Squad[/head]  

The following procedure must be strictly adhered to during the execution:  
1. The suspect is to be [bold]seated and blindfolded[/bold] in a chair.  
2. A minimum of [bold]3 security officers[/bold] shall form a firing line.  
3. The Head of Security will give the [bold]“Fire” command[/bold].  
4. All officers will open [bold]fire simultaneously[/bold] and continue until the suspect is confirmed deceased.  

Any deviation from these procedures will result in legal scrutiny from the Justice Department.  

The Head of Security is hereby required to oversee and ensure the proper execution of this order. Confirmation of compliance must be documented and submitted to the Justice Department immediately upon completion.  

[bold][italic]This order is final and binding.[/italic][/bold]
