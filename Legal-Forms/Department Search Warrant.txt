[color=#4e0041][head=0]Department Search Warrant[/head]

This warrant gives security permission to search a particular department.
[color=red][bold]This warrant must be approved and stamped by the dept. head of the department to be searched [italic]or[/italic] the highest ranking avaliable command (often Captain or HoP).[/bold][/color]

If illegal items are found in the searched department they will be confiscated and the owner of these items may be detained at the officers discretion. [bold]Legal items may not be taken in a search.[/bold]

[head=3]Conditions[/head]
1. The search must be conducted in accordance with NT Space Law §1-2.2.
2. Only authorized personnel may conduct the search.
3. Any evidence found must be documented and reported immediately.
4. This document is not valid unless stamped by at least one of the listed required personnel.
5. This form follows NT Space Law §1-2.2

By signing below, you acknowledge that the search is authorized under NT regulations and agree to adhere to all specified conditions.
